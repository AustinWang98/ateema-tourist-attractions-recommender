# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Chicago visitors, tourism operators, and Ateema stakeholders use this recommender to quickly browse ranked ChicagoDoes locations, tune a trip profile, and generate an itinerary from a verified place catalog.

## Product Purpose

The product turns ChicagoDoes visitor and location data into a polished recommendation workflow. Success means users can understand why places are worth considering, trust that catalog places have verified media, and move from browsing to a day plan without friction.

## Brand Personality

Confident, urban, editorial. The interface should feel like a premium city-planning console powered by real behavior, not a generic travel directory.

## Anti-references

Avoid pale admin dashboards, generic SaaS gray cards, over-white surfaces, random decorative color, and mismatched controls that make the results page feel unrelated to the landing page.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the city present through dark cinematic surfaces and verified media.
2. Use color semantically: amber for primary action and momentum, cyan/blue for catalog metadata, coral for hot spots.
3. Make the recommender feel trustworthy through consistent controls, stable card dimensions, and readable contrast.
4. Let photos lead, but keep ranking, categories, and itinerary controls immediately scannable.
5. Prefer restrained product density over marketing decoration after the user enters the tool.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for all text and controls. Preserve keyboard focus states, avoid relying on color alone for selected categories, and respect reduced-motion preferences.
